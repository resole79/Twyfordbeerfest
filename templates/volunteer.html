Interested in volunteering at our festival?

Please fill in the form adding the areas you feel you can help with and we will email you.
As a valued member of the team you have access to all areas and can try any of the beers and other drinks we have on offer.
As an added bonus you will aslo get free entry to the “Crazy Diamond” Gig held on Thursday 5th June.
Everybody gets fed and you will have plenty of breaks throughout the periods you are at work to enjoy a beer and watch the entertainment.
If you have any special skills then please let us know when we contact you.
Do not worry if you have not helped out at a festival before - we are a very friendly team and will mentor you.
Not poured a pint before?
Not a problem we will show you how!

I look forward to chatting with you.

Ian Wisdom
